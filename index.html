Introduction

Flow Accounting Software 

Flow Accounting is a designed for small and medium-sized businesses. It helps manage essential financial operations such as general ledger, accounts payable and receivable, inventory, purchasing, sales, and financial reporting through a simple, user-friendly, browser-based platform. Ideal for businesses seeking an affordable, efficient, and multi-user accounting solution.

This documentation is designed to help you get started quickly and make the most of your accounting software. Whether you are setting up your business for the first time or managing day-to-day financial operations, you will find clear guidance, step-by-step instructions, and best practices throughout this help center.
